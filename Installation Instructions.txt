dale_out_and_about
Tired of Monika not having any idea where you're taking her? Use this submod to be more specific in your destination!

To install: place in your DDLC /game directory, under the /Submods folder - newly created, if necessary.

Important: To get updates on this submod as they happen, you MUST have the Submod Updater Plugin! Otherwise you will be stuck on previous versions. Download this here: https://github.com/Booplicate/MAS-Submods-SubmodUpdaterPlugin
